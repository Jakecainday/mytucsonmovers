Yes, we specialize in handling delicate and valuable items. Our team is trained to safely move specialty items such as pianos, antiques, and artwork. We use specialized equipment and techniques to ensure these items are transported securely and without damage.

We take extensive precautions to protect your belongings during the move. Our team uses high-quality packing materials and moving blankets to safeguard your items. We also employ proper lifting techniques and secure everything in the moving truck to prevent damage during transport.

How far in advance should I schedule my move?

We recommend scheduling your move as early as possible to ensure availability. For local moves, booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance is generally sufficient. For long-distance or high-demand periods, such as summer or the start of a new semester, booking 1-2 months in advance is ideal.
